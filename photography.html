About

"The whole point of taking pictures is so that you don't have to explain things in Words."

Let's see you explain the theme of Reflux 2019 with your pictures of "Engineering Technology" and win amazing prizes.

Rules for Stage 1

Team participation is not allowed
  • A participant can upload only one entry
  • Your Photo should very well depict our topic "Engineering Technology"
  • You have to upload the picture on your Facebook account and tag reflux page and add hashtag #reflux_2019
  • Last date for the photo submission/uploading is 1st September
  • You will be rewarded points for number of likes and shares on photograph
  • Do not include credentials such as name/address etc. on the photographs
  • Watermarks are not allowed
  • Digital manipulation that distorts the reality of the photos will not be allowed.
  • Only basic enhancements such as sharpening, contrast adjustment, or simple cropping will be allowed.

  • Selected participants will have to come to IIT Guwahati for Reflux 2019 for stage 2
  • The details for stage 2 will be shared soon
