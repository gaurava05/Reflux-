Reflux presents “RESEARCH - MASTER 2019” the Paper and Poster Presentation competition of its 2019 edition with an objective to encourage talented and skilled young engineering students to showcase their creative original work in research domain. An open platform for presenting innovative ideas through research papers and competing tip to tip with the sharpest minds in the field of engineering. We wholeheartedly invite you to showcase your innovative ideas on this platform which will be judged by an experienced panel of Professors or Industrialists.
